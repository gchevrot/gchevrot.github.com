About me

~~ If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together. -- African Proverb ~~
I am a computational biophysicist (postdoc fellow) working at MEMPHYS - Center for Biomembrane Physics - at the University of Southern Denmark Currently I am in the biophysics group of H. Khandelia and I am using molecular dynamics to study membrane proteins. My research interests are in biomolecular simulation. I enjoy molecular simulations as a virtual microscope of molecules and as a powerful tool to explain and predict the properties of materials.
